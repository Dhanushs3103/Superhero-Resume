About me

I was a brilliant industrialist, living on the cutting edge of technology. During a weapons demonstration in a war-torn region, I was captured by insurgents. In captivity, I realized the devastation my creations had caused. Together with a fellow captive, Yinsen, I built a suit of armor to escape. This experience changed me. I transformed from a selfish genius into a hero, using my technological prowess to protect the innocent. That moment marked the birth of Iron Man. I chose to wield my creations for good, defending the world as both a symbol of redemption and a guardian against threats.
